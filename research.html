Theory and Application of Chiral Anomalies in Quantum Field Theory

Masters thesis · University of Texas at Austin · 2023

An investigation of how chiral anomalies emerge in quantum field theories and their implications for physical observables. Derives the U(1) axial anomaly via the path integral formalism and applies the result to calculate the lifetime of the η′ meson to within 2σ of experimental values reported by the Particle Data Group.

Operations Research: Optimal Task Assignment for AI Data Collection

Whitepaper · 2025

A bespoke linear programming model for maximizing data volume and quality in AI robotics data collection operations. Introduces a quality-scaled volume metric and demonstrates a 60% improvement over random assignment and 10% over the best greedy heuristic via Monte Carlo simulation.
